How it works
Three simple steps
From idea to shared wishlist in minutes — no app download needed
Step 1
Build your list
Add wishes for any occasion — birthday, Christmas, Mother's Day, or anything else. Add links, set priorities, and keep it updated any time.
Step 2
Create your groups
Organise people into groups — My Children, Siblings, Work Friends. Each group gets its own share code and sees only what you choose.
Step 3
Share and relax
Send your share code via WhatsApp, email, or text. Your people view your list, claim gifts, and you never get a duplicate present again.
Features
Everything you need
Simple but thoughtful — built around how real families actually give gifts
Private by design
You control who sees what. Your children and your work friends never see the same list unless you want them to.
Gift claiming
Viewers can tap "I'll buy this" to claim a gift. Everyone else sees it as taken — no more three people buying the same thing.
Any occasion
Birthday, Christmas, Mother's Day, Valentine's, or any occasion. Keep separate lists for each or mix them together.
Works on any device
No app download needed. Works in any browser on any phone, tablet, or computer. Your data syncs across all your devices.
Add item links
Link directly to the item on Amazon, ASOS, or anywhere else. No more vague descriptions — gift buyers know exactly what to get.
Totally free
OurSpecialList is completely free to use. No subscription, no credit card, no limits on wishes or groups.
